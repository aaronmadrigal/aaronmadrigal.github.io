ABOUT ME

My name is Aaron. I love directing, producing and editing videos. I live in Southern California. I am also studying computer engineering at Cal Poly Pomona. I work as a video producer / IT student assistant at Cal Poly Pomona's video production "Media Vision". I aim to be an engineer or producer in the entertainment / film industry. My love for making videos began in 6th grade creating gaming videos. My passion for content creation has grown in the fields of filmmaking, short films and engineering. I still enjoy making videos on my YouTube channel for nearly a decade.
